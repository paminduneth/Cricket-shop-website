Cricket Safety Guidline

  1. Protective Equipment:
    • Batsmen should wear helmets with face guards.
    • Batsmen and wicketkeepers should wear appropriate padding, including leg guards, thigh guards, and chest protectors.
    • Fielders in close positions may wear helmets and other protective gear.
  2. First Aid and Medical Support:
    • Ensure first aid equipment is readily available.
    • Medical personnel should be on standby during matches.
    • Establish protocols for dealing with injuries, including concussion protocols.
